About

I am a researcher, facilitator, and policy expert currently at the OECD Centre for Entrepreneurship, SMEs, Regions and Cities.

My work spans multiple disciplines, drawing connections between industries and countries to drive effective governance practices. I believe that the most important lever for change is policy, and I am dedicated to making a difference through evidence-based policy analysis and innovation.

I have served in three different parts of the OECD: its Centre for Entrepreneurship, SMEs, Regions and Cities; the Public Governance Directorate; and the Economics Department.
